About

My name is Krzysztof, and I’ve been programming my whole life.

Before iPhone came out I used to work on games and graphics programming.

I started programming 20 years ago, when I was 8 years old.

You’re probably thinking “Programming in Primary School? What did you write, hello world?”

Actually, I wrote Game Engines, Physics Frameworks and my own User Interface Libraries.

I still have projects from my Primary school in my portfolio.

I was the Lead App Engineer at The App Business in London, where I was running a team of over 10 engineers, ensuring we deliver best quality projects for big clients like Unilever or News International.

I enjoy sharing my knowledge and experience with others, as such I’ve created a lot of tools and libraries (Including Playgrounds for Objective-C and Swift) and made them open source which you’ll find on Github.

You can also find me speaking at technical conferences around the world.

I’m always striving to improve my productivity, and take great pride in my professional craftsmanship, you can read how I work with clients.

If you’d like to learn more about my life story, you can listen to: Debug, NSBrief or Cocos2d Podcasts.

I don’t do subcontracting for agencies.

I’m available for iOS consulting.
